Journey of the Little Mermaid

This was the first slow moving attraction ride I have ever been on. While this movie is not necessarily anywhere on my list of favorites. Instead, The Little Mermaid means a lot to my mom, therefore, I relate this series to her, bringing me a sense of familiarity. I sent the photos and videos to my mom back in Az and she told me that she really wants to go there. She said that just the videos alone made her cry.

Web Slingers

One of the more packed lines. Inside, the attraction uses very high quality screens to display Spiderman and robots, the details were rather realistic and convincing. After watching a short "performance", riders are guided to the actual rides. We equip special glasses that help us see the 3d effect of the ride. Our hands are motion tracked and thats when the fun starts; the car makes way to several different screens where we use our motion tracked hands to "shoot webs" at the interactive screen.

Millennium Falcon: Smugglers Run

I am not a Star Wars fan. Not that I dislike it, more like it never caught my attention so I never cared for it. The outside of this attraction looked really interesting and we wanted to give it a try since the line and wait time was short. It took us a very long time to walk to the inside of the building where the line actually begins. We joked that the walk to the line was the actual attraction. We waited a couple of minutes until the speaker turn on and told the crowd that the wait time was increased by 30 minutes. We felt lied to. Once the line diminished, we were taken inside the Millennium Falcon. It was huge and really caught my attention. Guests were separated into parties of 4 since it takes 4 people to play. Things took a twist when it was my party's turn. Two of the people decided to not come along me and my girlfriend and left us alone inside the game. When a four player game is played with only two people, you can tell how it went. The inside was very detailed. The screen, seats, sounds, and everything was immersive. Aside from inside, it was one the worst attractions and frankly a waste of time and money
